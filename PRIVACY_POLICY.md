# Privacy Policy - Pulsara Kiosk

**Last Updated:** June 2024

## Overview
Pulsara Kiosk is an attendance management application that processes facial recognition data for workplace attendance tracking.

## Information We Collect
1. **Facial Recognition Data**: Camera images are processed locally on your device using TensorFlow Lite models
2. **Employee Information**: Name, ID, and attendance records
3. **Device Information**: Model, OS version, for performance monitoring
4. **Network Information**: Connection status for sync purposes

## How We Use Your Data
- Facial data is used only for attendance verification
- Attendance records are stored locally and synced to our secure servers
- Data is never shared with third parties except as required by law

## Data Storage & Security
- Facial recognition models run locally on your device (no cloud processing)
- All data transmissions use HTTPS encryption
- Sensitive information is encrypted at rest using industry-standard algorithms

## Data Retention
- Facial recognition data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored on device
- Attendance records are retained as per your organization's policy
- You can request deletion of your data through your administrator

## Permissions We Request
- **Camera**: Required for facial recognition
- **Internet**: Required to sync attendance data
- **Network State**: To detect connectivity
- **Notifications**: For attendance alerts

## Contact
For privacy concerns, contact your organization's administrator or our support team.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes.
